Known Planet Walks


Planet Walk Fairbanks, AL This 1:4.4 billion scale model of the solar system. It will take you on a little less than one mile walk through our solar system.

Planet Walk Lansing,MIA 5.5-mile journey, starts outside Impression 5 Science Center, winds along the River Trail and ends at Abrams Planetarium on Michigan State University's campus.

Planet Walk Sugarloaf Ridge State Parkashrunk the solar system more than 2,360,000,000 times, small enough for the park to include the orbit of distant Pluto, and large enough that the smallest planets could still be seen.

Planet Walk Somerset, England is a sculpture trail model of the Solar System, located in Somerset, England. The model uses the towpath of the 22-kilometre (14-mile) Bridgwater and Taunton Canal to display a model of the Sun and its planets in their proportionally correct sizes and distances apart. Unusually for a Solar System model, there are two sets of planets, so that the diameter of the orbits is represented.

Planet Walk NC Science Festival Discover the relative sizes and distances of objects in our solar system

Planet Walk Brooke Pioneer Tail Trail has been repaved, only the sun, neptune, pluto are left. The volunteers have it on their list to repaint the walk.
